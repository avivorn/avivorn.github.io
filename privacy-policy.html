Privacy Policy

Gladiator Arena Manager

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Developer: Avivorn · Contact: avivorn@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Gladiator Arena Manager handles information when you use the App. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information stored on your device

The App is a single-player game that does not require an account, login, or registration. Your game progress — including save slots, rosters, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to this game data, and it is removed if you uninstall the App.

2. Information collected by advertising partners

The App displays optional rewarded advertisements to support development. These ads are provided by third-party advertising networks, specifically:

  • Unity Ads (Unity Technologies)
  • Google AdMob (Google LLC)

To serve and measure ads, these partners may automatically collect information such as your device's advertising identifier (e.g. Google Advertising ID), IP address, device type and operating system, and general usage and interaction data. This information is collected and processed by those partners under their own privacy policies, not by us:

  • Unity privacy policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google / AdMob privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. How information is used

Information collected by our advertising partners is used to deliver advertisements (personalized or non-personalized depending on your settings and consent), to limit how often you see the same ad, to measure ad performance, and to detect fraud and abuse. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Your choices

  • Advertising ID: You can reset or delete your advertising identifier, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).
  • Rewarded ads are optional: Watching an ad is always your choice and is never required to continue playing.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). The App does not include account registration, login, chat, or any forms through which users can submit personal information. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App's advertising partners, please contact us.

6. Data retention & security

We do not store your personal data on our own servers. Game data remains on your device until you delete it. Any data handled by advertising partners is retained according to their respective policies. We rely on the security measures of the platforms (Google Play) and ad networks that process this information.

7. International users

The App is available internationally. Information collected by our advertising partners may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, under the safeguards described in their privacy policies.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location (for example, under the EU GDPR or California's CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. Because the App does not require an account or login, we are generally unable to identify individual users on our own. For information collected by our advertising partners, please refer to their privacy policies linked above. You may also contact us at the email below and we will assist where we can.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at avivorn@gmail.com.
