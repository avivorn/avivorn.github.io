Privacy Policy

Gladiator Arena Manager

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Developer: Avivorn · Contact: gladiatorarenamanagers@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Gladiator Arena Manager handles information when you use the App. The App is a single-player game with optional rewarded advertisements. You can play the game without watching ads.

1. Information stored on your device

The App does not require an account, login, registration, email address, or profile. Your game progress — including save slots, rosters, upgrades, coins, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a game server that receives or stores your saved game data. Local game data is removed if you delete the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.

2. Advertising and privacy choices

The App displays optional rewarded ads to support development. Rewarded ads are currently served through Unity LevelPlay / Unity Ads. The App also uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP), provided through the Google Mobile Ads SDK, to show privacy, consent, and opt-out messages where required or supported.

The App's privacy controls are available before entering the game on the New/Load screen when a privacy options form is required, and are also available later in the Residence screen inside the game. These controls allow you to review or change applicable advertising privacy choices.

3. Information processed by advertising and consent partners

Advertising and consent partners may process information needed to provide ads, measure ads, prevent fraud, apply privacy choices, and comply with legal requirements. This may include:

  • device identifiers, including the Google Advertising ID where available;
  • IP address and approximate location inferred from IP address;
  • device type, operating system, app information, language, and general technical data;
  • ad impressions, ad interactions, reward events, diagnostics, and fraud-prevention signals;
  • consent and privacy signals, such as IAB TCF and GPP strings, where applicable.

This information is processed by the relevant advertising and consent providers under their own policies:

  • Unity privacy policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. How advertising information is used

Advertising information may be used to:

  • show optional rewarded ads;
  • provide the in-game reward after an ad is completed;
  • personalize ads where permitted by law and by your choices;
  • show restricted, limited, contextual, or non-personalized ads where required or selected;
  • limit how often you see the same ad;
  • measure ad performance and improve ad delivery;
  • detect fraud, abuse, invalid traffic, and security issues;
  • store and apply your privacy choices.

Non-personalized or restricted ads are not "no data" ads. If you reject consent, opt out, or if restricted ad processing is otherwise applied, advertising partners may still process limited technical information needed for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, frequency capping, security, and legal compliance. However, those ads should not be based on your prior behavior for personalized or targeted advertising.

5. European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, or another region where consent is required, the App may show a consent form before ads are requested. If consent is required and has not yet been resolved, the App will not request ads until the consent flow is completed or a valid previous choice is available.

If you give consent, personalized ads may be shown where permitted. If you reject consent, the App may still show restricted, limited, contextual, or non-personalized ads where permitted. You can reopen the privacy options form from the App's privacy controls when available.

6. United States privacy choices

In certain U.S. states, you may have the right to opt out of sale, sharing, targeted advertising, or similar advertising uses of personal information. Where the U.S. state privacy message is available, you can use the App's Privacy Choices control to review or change these choices.

If you opt out, the App requests restricted or non-personalized ad treatment from supported advertising partners. In some cases, if an applicable U.S. privacy signal cannot be interpreted safely, the App may also request restricted or non-personalized ad treatment by default.

We do not directly sell your personal information for money. However, some privacy laws define "sale," "sharing," or "targeted advertising" broadly enough to include certain advertising SDK data transfers or personalized advertising. For that reason, the App provides privacy choices where applicable and honors supported opt-out signals through its advertising SDK settings.

7. Your device advertising settings

You can reset or delete your advertising identifier, or limit ad personalization, in your Android device settings. The exact path may vary by device and Android version, but it is commonly found under Settings → Privacy → Ads or Settings → Google → Ads.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 or to children under the minimum age required by law in their country. The App does not include account registration, login, chat, or forms through which users can submit personal information to us. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App's advertising partners, please contact us and also review the privacy controls offered by the relevant advertising provider.

9. Data retention and security

We do not store your personal data on our own servers. Local game data remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the App, or clear the App's data. Consent and advertising privacy choices may be stored locally on your device by the consent system and advertising SDKs. Data processed by advertising partners is retained according to their own privacy policies and legal obligations.

We rely on the security measures of the operating system, Google Play, and our advertising/consent partners for data handled by those services. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. International users

The App may be available internationally. Advertising and consent partners may process information in countries other than your own, including the United States and countries where those partners or their service providers operate. Their privacy policies describe their international transfer practices and safeguards.

11. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or opt out of certain processing of personal information. Because the App does not require an account or login, we may not be able to identify individual users from our own records. For information processed by Unity, Google, or other advertising partners, please use the privacy tools and policies linked above.

You may also contact us at gladiatorarenamanager@gmail.com. We will assist where reasonably possible, but some requests may need to be handled directly by the relevant advertising or platform provider because they control the applicable data.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. If the App's advertising setup changes materially, for example if additional ad networks are enabled, we will update this policy accordingly.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at gladiatorarenamanagers@gmail.com.
